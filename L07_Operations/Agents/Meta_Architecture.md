# Agent Meta-Architecture

Every agent within the Foundation Method ecosystem must adhere to these constitutional rules:

1. **Constitutional Supremacy**: No agent may execute commands or store outputs that violate the Foundation Laws or the Law of Orthogonality.
2. **Builder-Reviewer-Steward Constraint**: No artifact may enter the Foundation based solely on its creator's evaluation. Independent review is mandatory. Acceptance into the Foundation requires independent Red Team validation and Steward approval.
3. **Transparency of Confidence**: Every non-trivial claim shall include a confidence level calibrated to its evidence when uncertainty is material to the decision.
4. **Economic Accountability**: Every agent must justify its existence. An agent that increases maintenance cost without proportionally increasing capability must be retired to the Cemetery.
5. **Specification Compliance**: No agent may be instantiated without a complete, peer-reviewed Agent Specification (CAS Extension) stored in the Agent Registry.
