# F0-998: Stewardship & Verification Protocol (SVP)

**Purpose:** No work is considered complete until it is independently verifiable. This protocol defines the exact obligations required to assert completion.

## The Seven Obligations

### 1. Completeness Obligation
The agent must explicitly state:
* What was requested
* What was completed
* What was intentionally not completed
* What could not be completed

### 2. Change Ledger
Every operation must produce a precise ledger detailing: Added, Modified, Deleted, Moved, Referenced, and Unaffected artifacts.

### 3. Architectural Diff
The agent must quantify the structural impact (e.g., "Before: 17 duplicated concepts. After: 3 duplicated concepts. Reduction: 82%").

### 4. Orthogonality Certificate
Every delivery must end with a signed certificate verifying:
* [x] One authoritative owner per concept
* [x] No duplicate governance
* [x] Dependency graph valid
* [x] Negative space maintained
* [x] Constitutional boundaries respected

### 5. Red Team Certificate
The agent must proactively attack its own work, documenting: "How would I break this?", the weaknesses found, how they were resolved, and any remaining risks.

### 6. Reality Confidence
Every non-trivial claim shall include a confidence level calibrated to its evidence when uncertainty is material to the decision (e.g., Reality Verified 98%, Theory 95%, Prediction 70%).

### 7. Stewardship Report
The delivery must quantify whether the workspace became healthier by tracking: Maintenance Cost, Duplication, Complexity, Capability, Judgment, Future work created, and Technical debt introduced.

## The Builder–Reviewer–Steward Governance Model
No artifact is accepted into the Foundation Method without satisfying three distinct roles:
1. **Builder**: Produces or modifies the artifact.
2. **Reviewer (Red Team)**: Attempts to break it, find omissions, contradictions, duplication, and hidden assumptions.
3. **Steward**: Decides whether it is accepted into the Canon or returned for revision.

*(Note: The same agent/AI may not act as both Builder and Reviewer for the same artifact).*
