# Architecture Decision Record (ADR) Policy

**Invariant:** Every architectural change must be preceded by an Architecture Decision Record (ADR).

## Categories
All ADRs must be tagged with one of the following categories:
- **Repository**: Structure, hosting, branching strategies.
- **Protocol**: Changes to the recovery or verification method.
- **Specification**: Amendments to the FZS itself.
- **Architecture**: Orchestration, pipelines, interfaces.
- **Governance**: Rights, rules, policy changes.
- **Tooling**: CI, automation, scripts.
