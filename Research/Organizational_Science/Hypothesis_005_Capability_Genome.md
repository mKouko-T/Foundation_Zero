# Hypothesis 005: The Capability Genome

**Status**: Experimental / Scientific Hypothesis  
**Domain**: Organizational Intelligence Engineering  
**Premise**: Every organizational capability (e.g., Marketing Intelligence, Collections, Lead Routing) consists of a recurring, invariant set of "genes." Marketing is not fundamentally different from Finance; it is simply a different arrangement of the same capability genome.

## The Invariant Genes

Every capability in any organization is hypothesized to contain:
1. **Purpose**: The reason the capability exists.
2. **Owner**: The business actor accountable for its success.
3. **Inputs**: The resources and data consumed.
4. **Outputs**: The value or information produced.
5. **Decisions**: The resource allocations it initiates or supports.
6. **Rules**: The logic governing execution.
7. **Knowledge**: The stored assumptions and context.
8. **Information**: The real-time signals flowing through it.
9. **Constraints**: The limits on its execution (legal, financial, physical).
10. **Customers**: The consumers of its output.
11. **Suppliers**: The providers of its inputs.
12. **Measurements**: The KPIs and leading/lagging indicators.
13. **Interfaces**: Connections to other capabilities.
14. **Technology**: The temporary systems supporting execution.
15. **Economics**: The money, time, attention, risk, and opportunity cost consumed.
16. **Risks**: The failure modes and entropy generation.
17. **Improvement Loop**: The mechanism for learning from outcomes.
18. **AI Opportunity**: Potential for AI assistance.
19. **Autonomy Opportunity**: Potential for fully autonomous execution.
