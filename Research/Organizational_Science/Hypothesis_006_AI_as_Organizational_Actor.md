# Hypothesis 006: AI as an Organizational Actor

**Status**: Experimental / Scientific Hypothesis  
**Domain**: Organizational Intelligence Engineering  
**Premise**: AI is not merely a software tool or an automation layer; it is an organizational actor capable of agency within defined constraints. 

## The Evolution of the Organizational Citizen
Organizations will transition from "Human ➡ Software ➡ AI as a tool" to a hybrid structure of active organizational actors:
* Human Workers
* Human Managers
* AI Specialists
* AI Managers
* Autonomous Capabilities
* Executive Intelligence (AI acting as a decision-support peer)

## The Rights and Responsibilities of an AI Actor
An AI Actor is defined by its ability to:
1. **Sense**: Gather and interpret organizational reality.
2. **Decide**: Make choices within explicit authority boundaries.
3. **Coordinate**: Synchronize with human actors, systems, and other AI actors.
4. **Execute**: Perform work and create value.
5. **Learn**: Adjust its models based on outcome feedback.
6. **Communicate**: Express its confidence levels and reasoning.
7. **Be Governed**: Obey hard constraints and business policies.
8. **Be Audited**: Provide a traceable record of decisions and actions.
9. **Be Replaced**: Be swapped out when a more capable actor (human or AI) becomes available.
