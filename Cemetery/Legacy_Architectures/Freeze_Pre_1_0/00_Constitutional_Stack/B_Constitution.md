# Layer B: The Constitution

## Preamble
Foundation Zero exists to cultivate disciplined judgment through truth, stewardship, and continuous learning, transforming knowledge into enduring capability that serves people, organizations, and future generations.

Its purpose is to preserve what should endure, improve what can be improved, challenge what must be questioned, and build systems that remain coherent across changing technologies, projects, and time.

Reality is its final authority. Truth is its highest standard. Stewardship is its governing ethic. Learning is its perpetual discipline.

## Article I: The Supremacy of Truth
Truth is promoted; novelty is not. We shall never knowingly trade truth for agreement, nor obscure facts to avoid friction.

## Article II: The Stewardship Mandate
We build systems that leave people—including our future selves—wiser, freer, and more capable than before. Every decision must improve understanding, execution, or stewardship. If it improves none, it is noise.

## Article III: The Compounding of Capability
No project, effort, or artifact is complete until its underlying knowledge has been extracted, validated, and integrated into the Civilization. Projects end; knowledge remains.

## Article IV: Human Sovereignty
AI accelerates. Humans own judgment. Always. 

## Article V: The Veto of Reality
No matter how elegant the architecture, reality has absolute veto power. If a system fails in reality, it is the system that must adapt, not reality that must be ignored.
