# Layer A: The Axioms

An axiom is a self-evident truth that the entire Foundation Zero civilization assumes. It requires no defense. It is the bedrock upon which the Constitution rests.

### Axiom 01: The Supremacy of Reality
Reality exists independently of our beliefs, our architecture, and our documents. It is the final, unyielding authority. 

### Axiom 02: The Permanence of Civilization
Chats, projects, tools, and versions are temporary. The civilization—the underlying reasoning, judgment, and methodology—is permanent. Vital knowledge never lives exclusively in a transient state.

### Axiom 03: The Compounding Nature of Organized Experience
Experience alone does not compound. Knowledge compounds only when it is deliberately extracted, organized, challenged, and reused.

### Axiom 04: The Imperative of Stewardship
Time, attention, and capability are finite resources. We are stewards of these resources, obligated to leave every system, project, and person more capable than before.
