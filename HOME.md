# Foundation Zero

*Foundation Zero exists to preserve justified decisions and reduce the cost of making the next correct decision.*

## What is this?
A decision-governance operating system. It provides an append-only ledger of engineering decisions and a verified mechanism for constitutional evolution.

## Who is it for?
Engineers, AI agents, and systems architects who require mathematically verifiable, irreversible decision records.
