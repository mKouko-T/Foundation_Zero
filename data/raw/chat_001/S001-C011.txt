My Final Recommendation (Locked)
For every serious project we build—from your Organizational Effectiveness System to Stories & Steps, to future SaaS products—I recommend adopting a three-layer governance model:
1.	Project Constitution (PC) — the enduring identity, philosophy, invariants, and governance of the project. Changes are rare and require deliberate review.
2.	Project Operating System (POS) — the canonical operational blueprint: architecture, methodology, decision ledger, cognitive operating procedures, evolution rules, and execution model. This is the project's living operating system.
3.	Project State (PS) — the current snapshot: active work, open questions, blockers, priorities, and next steps. This changes continuously.
This separation keeps timeless truths, operational logic, and day-to-day progress from becoming entangled, dramatically reducing future reconstruction cost.
