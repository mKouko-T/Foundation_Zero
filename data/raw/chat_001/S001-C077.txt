Where Autonomous AI Fits
This is another area where I think we can go much further than typical companies.
Our goal should not be automation whenever we can achieve autonomy.
Think in maturity levels:
1.	Manual – People perform the work.
2.	Standardized – SOPs and checklists make the work consistent.
3.	Digitized – Systems capture the work.
4.	Automated – Rules execute repetitive tasks.
5.	Augmented – AI assists people with recommendations.
6.	Autonomous – AI agents independently perform bounded tasks, escalate exceptions, and continuously improve using feedback.
7.	Adaptive Organization – Multiple specialized agents coordinate with people to sense, decide, act, learn, and optimize across the enterprise.
Whenever we redesign a process, we'll ask:
•	Can this step be eliminated?
•	If not, can it be standardized?
•	If standardized, can it be automated?
•	If automated, can it become autonomous with appropriate governance?
