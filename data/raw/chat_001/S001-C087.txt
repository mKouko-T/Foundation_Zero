# Observation 001: The Intervention Budget
**Status**: Operational Observation  
**Domain**: Organizational Intelligence Engineering  
**Premise**: Organizations can only absorb a finite amount of change. Every intervention (meeting, dashboard, workflow, automation, AI agent, policy) consumes organizational energy in the form of attention, trust, money, political capital, and change capacity.
## The Optimization Function
Organizational Intelligence Engineering should ultimately optimize for:
**Value Created per Unit of Organizational Disruption.**
## Practical Implications
* Sometimes a tiny Excel automation beats a million-dollar ERP customization.
* Sometimes deleting a report creates more value than adding five dashboards.
* An intervention that is intellectually perfect but bankrupts the organization's change capacity is an engineering failure. 
*Note: This observation will remain an active observation until repeated execution proves it should become a formal law of OIE.*
