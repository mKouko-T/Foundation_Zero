Job Purpose
Position Title: Operations Performance Lead
Purpose of the Role
The Operations Performance Lead is responsible for improving operational visibility, performance measurement, process effectiveness, and cross-functional coordination across the organization. The role supports executive decision-making by transforming operational data into actionable insights, ensuring reliable reporting, strengthening business processes, and driving system adoption and continuous improvement initiatives.
The role serves as a bridge between Operations, Sales, Finance, and Technology to ensure that leadership has accurate information, effective processes, and clear performance indicators to support growth and operational excellence.
Key Responsibilities
1.	Performance Management & Reporting
• Design and maintain operational and commercial KPIs.
• Develop dashboards and reporting frameworks for executive management.
• Monitor business performance and identify trends, risks, and opportunities.
• Provide regular performance reviews and recommendations to leadership.
2.	Process Improvement
• Analyze operational workflows and identify bottlenecks.
• Recommend and implement process improvements.
• Support the development and documentation of standard operating procedures.
• Promote operational efficiency and consistency across departments.
3.	Systems & CRM Excellence
• Support CRM implementation, adoption, and continuous improvement.
• Ensure data quality, process compliance, and reporting accuracy.
• Coordinate with system owners to improve operational visibility and usability.
• Assist in maximizing the value of Odoo and other operational systems.
4.	Cross-Functional Coordination
• Work closely with Sales, Finance, Customer Service, Projects, and Operations teams.
• Facilitate alignment between departments on processes, data, and performance objectives.
• Support company-wide operational initiatives and improvement projects.
5.	Executive Decision Support
• Provide analytical support to the CEO, COO, CFO, and Operations Director.
• Prepare decision-support materials, business cases, forecasts, and operational analyses.
• Identify root causes of performance issues and propose corrective actions.
• Support strategic and operational planning activities.
Success Measures
• Improved visibility of business performance.
• Increased reliability and adoption of operational reporting.
• Successful CRM and system utilization.
• Reduction of operational inefficiencies and process bottlenecks.
• Faster and more informed executive decision-making.
• Strong cross-functional collaboration and accountability.
Career Progression Path
Operations Performance Lead
→ Senior Operations Performance Lead
→ Head of Operations
→ Operations Director
