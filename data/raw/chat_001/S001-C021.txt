Adversarial Audit: F0-002 Constitution
The Attack Vector
You correctly identified the vulnerability. A Constitution that mandates "Truth" and "Reality" but fails to define how we know what is true or real is susceptible to internal collapse. If two stewards disagree on what reality is signaling, the current 16 Articles provide no mechanism to resolve the dispute other than appeals to authority, which Article III explicitly forbids.

I have attacked the proposed 16 Articles. Here is where the Constitution breaks:

Vulnerability 1: The Burden of Proof Loophole
The Exploit: Article IV mandates distinguishing between hypothesis and evidence, but it does not specify who must provide the evidence. A steward could introduce a massive architectural assumption and force the system to spend resources disproving it.
The Break: The Constitution allows "guilty until proven innocent" for new ideas, draining cognitive load.
Vulnerability 2: The Unfalsifiable Trap
The Exploit: Article III states reality is the final validator. However, if a steward introduces a theory that cannot be tested against reality (e.g., "This architecture is better because it feels cleaner"), reality cannot veto it.
The Break: Subjectivity masquerades as reality because the claim is structurally immune to testing.
Vulnerability 3: The Contradiction Paradox
The Exploit: Article X states "No contradiction shall remain unresolved." But what happens when we lack the information to resolve it?
The Break: The system is forced to make a premature, unevidenced decision simply to satisfy the coherence mandate, thereby violating Article IV (Truth).
Vulnerability 4: The Map vs. Territory Confusion
The Exploit: The Constitution demands Truth but fails to explicitly acknowledge that all human/AI representations of reality are merely models.
The Break: Stewards will inevitably defend a model (a standard or system) as if it were reality itself, leading to dogmatism.
The Proposed Constitutional Amendments (Epistemology)
To seal these vulnerabilities, I propose injecting a new subset of Articles directly after Article IV (Truth).

PROPOSED: ARTICLE V - Epistemology & Epistemic Humility
The map is not the territory. All documents, frameworks, and architectures are models of reality, not reality itself. Models shall be updated when reality contradicts them. No model shall be defended against evidence.

PROPOSED: ARTICLE VI - The Burden of Proof
The burden of proof rests entirely upon the party making the claim, proposing the change, or introducing the artifact. The Method is never obligated to disprove an unevidenced assertion. What is asserted without evidence may be dismissed without evidence.
