1.	Manual

3.	Digitized

•	Inventory synchronization → Autonomous
•	Data quality monitoring → Autonomous
•	Exception detection → Autonomous
•	Root cause investigation → AI-assisted
•	Organizational recommendations → AI-assisted
•	Organizational governance changes → Human
