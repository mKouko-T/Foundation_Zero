# The Quality System
Excellence is not measured by adjectives like "military-grade" or "maximum." Excellence is an objective standard. Every major artifact produced by this infrastructure must satisfy five dimensions.
## The 5 Dimensions of Finished Work
1. **Truth**: Factually and logically sound. Supported by explicit epistemology and evidence ledgers.
2. **Coherence**: Internally consistent and architecturally aligned with the Constitution and Invariants.
3. **Utility**: Genuinely useful in real execution. Solves the problem it was designed to solve.
4. **Transferability**: Reusable beyond one specific situation, project, or company.
5. **Stewardship**: Sustainable and aligned with the life Maged wants to build. It must not cost faith, family, or integrity.
If an artifact scores poorly in any one dimension, **it is not finished**. We return to the drawing board or execute the `Challenge` command.

# Conversation Continuity Protocol
The chat is merely a working memory. The project is the permanent state. This protocol ensures we never lose continuity, never waste tokens, and never accidentally drift from the project's principles when moving between AI contexts.
## The Conversation Bootstrap Package (CBP)
Instead of reloading entire histories, every new conversation begins with a CBP.
**Structure of a CBP:**
1. **Identity**: Which project are we in?
2. **Current Phase**: Where are we in the maturity model?
3. **Locked Decisions**: Truths that are not to be rediscovered.
4. **Current Frontier**: What problem are we currently solving?
5. **Open Questions**: Where do we continue from here?
6. **Required Thinking Style**: The Cognitive Role the AI should assume.
7. **References**: Pointers to the Constitution, Operating System, and State.
## The Continuity Workflow
Every time a chat grows large or reaches a meaningful milestone, we execute this sequence:
1. **Sync**: Reconcile the latest validated knowledge into the project artifacts (Constitution, Operating System, Decision Ledger).
2. **Checkpoint**: Generate a fresh Conversation Bootstrap Package (e.g., `CBP_v7.md`) in `07_Conversation_Bootstrap/`.
3. **Open New Chat**: Discard the old working memory.
4. **Continue**: Load the CBP and immediately resume work at the frontier.
This separation keeps timeless truths, operational logic, and day-to-day progress perfectly insulated from conversational entropy.

# Decision Quality Standards
Every decision recorded in the Ledger must answer the following critical dimensions to maintain intellectual honesty and provide context for future audits.
## Decision Record Format
For every major conclusion or architectural choice:
* **Decision**: What did we decide?
* **Confidence Level**:
  * **A**: Proven repeatedly
  * **B**: Strong confidence
  * **C**: Working hypothesis
  * **D**: Experimental
  * **E**: Speculation
* **Evidence Ledger**: Why do we believe this? (Experience, Data, Scientific literature, Logical deduction, etc. Must map to the Epistemology trust levels).
* **Reversibility**: How difficult is this decision to undo? (High, Medium, Low)
* **Cost of Reversal**: What breaks if we change this later?
* **Unknowns**: What data or context are we missing at the time of this decision?
* **Kill Criteria**: Under what exact conditions should this decision be abandoned? (e.g., "If X occurs within 6 months, we reopen the architecture.")
* **Future Review Trigger**: A specific date, milestone, or event that forces a review of this decision.

Foundation Zero exists to cultivate disciplined judgment through truth, stewardship, and continuous learning, transforming knowledge into enduring capability that serves people, organizations, and future generations.

## Article III: The Compounding of Capability
No project, effort, or artifact is complete until its underlying knowledge has been extracted, validated, and integrated into the Civilization. Projects end; knowledge remains.
## Article IV: Human Sovereignty
AI accelerates. Humans own judgment. Always. 
## Article V: The Veto of Reality
No matter how elegant the architecture, reality has absolute veto power. If a system fails in reality, it is the system that must adapt, not reality that must be ignored.

# Layer C: Foundational Principles
