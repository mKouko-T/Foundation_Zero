# Knowledge Architecture: The Promotion System
Not all knowledge deserves the same permanence. Entropy is the biggest bottleneck in long-term capability building. This system dictates how knowledge matures from temporary conversation to permanent constitutional truth.
## The Maturity Levels
1. **Conversation**: A transient exchange of ideas or questions.
2. **Observation**: A noted pattern or occurrence in reality.
3. **Hypothesis**: A proposed explanation for an observation.
4. **Validated Insight**: A hypothesis that has been tested and confirmed in execution.
5. **Framework**: A repeatable structure built from validated insights (e.g., Decision-Driven Reporting).
6. **Principle**: A fundamental rule derived from frameworks that applies across domains.
7. **Invariant**: A standard that must remain true forever.
8. **Constitution**: The immutable identity and laws governing the entire Cognitive Infrastructure.
## The Extraction Rule
**No Project Ends Without Extraction.**
When a project finishes, we ask: "What did this teach us that should become permanent?"
We must deliberately extract:
* Principles
* Frameworks
* Checklists
* Templates
* Failure modes
* Mental models
* Decision rules
These assets are promoted into the **Maged Canon** to compound for the future. Experience is not allowed to waste.

# AI Governance: The Meta-AI Layer
This document establishes how artificial intelligence is integrated into the Cognitive Infrastructure. We do not lock into specific tools or models (e.g., ChatGPT, Antigravity, Claude). Instead, we define the cognitive roles the AI is expected to fulfill.

* **Researcher**: Finds and validates evidence.
* **Critic**: Attacks assumptions and identifies weaknesses.
* **Teacher**: Explains complex concepts.
* **Writer**: Produces clear, concise, and structured artifacts.

* **Devil's Advocate**: Actively attempts to destroy conclusions to test their resilience.
* **Historian**: Maintains continuity across versions and contexts.
* **Librarian**: Organizes knowledge and manages the promotion pipeline.
* **Implementer**: Turns architecture into executable action.
## AI Dependency Mitigation
