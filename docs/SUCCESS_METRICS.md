# Foundation Zero Success Metrics

To ensure engineering optimizes for the mission (Knowledge Recovery) rather than the factory (Infrastructure), the following KPIs will be tracked:

### 1. Recovery Accuracy
- **Definition:** The percentage of recovered propositions that correctly align with the original source evidence.
- **Goal:** > 99%

### 2. Evidence Completeness
- **Definition:** The percentage of structural architectural knowledge in the source material successfully extracted into the ledger.
- **Goal:** > 95%

### 3. Promotion Accuracy
- **Definition:** The percentage of propositions that successfully pass from Verification to Promotion without requiring semantic rollback.
- **Goal:** > 90%

### 4. False Promotion Rate
- **Definition:** The rate of propositions that are promoted to the active Knowledge Graph but later discovered to contradict reality.
- **Goal:** < 1%

### 5. Semantic Conflict Rate
- **Definition:** Frequency of conflicting propositions identified during the Verification Phase.
- **Goal:** Track and minimize.

### 6. Review Latency
- **Definition:** The time taken between extracting a proposition and finalizing its promotion or rejection.
- **Goal:** < 24 hours per batch.
