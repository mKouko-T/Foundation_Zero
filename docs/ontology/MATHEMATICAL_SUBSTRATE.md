# The Meta-Model (Level 1)

This substrate powers the Domain Ontologies (Level 2). It provides the mathematical calculus of the system without defining domain-specific knowledge.

## 1. The Three Irreducible Primitives
Everything emerges from these three concepts.
1. **Identity**: The absolute, mathematically verifiable anchor (*WHO*). Identity exists independent of meaning or physical representation.
2. **State**: The physical, cryptographic, or epistemic condition bound to an Identity at a specific temporal or version coordinate (*CURRENT CONDITION*).
3. **Relation**: A directional edge connecting Identities. 

*Transformations* and *Contexts* are not fundamental primitives; they are emergent. A Transformation is simply a state transition. A Context is simply a specialized Relation constraining an evaluation.

## 2. Orthogonal Facets (Trait Spaces)
Identities do not possess monolithic roles. An Identity's State is classified across orthogonal mathematical dimensions.
- **Epistemic Facet**: (Assertion, Observation, Claim)
- **Operational Facet**: (Specification, Capability, Project)
- **Lifecycle Facet**: (Draft, Verified, Deprecated)
- **Governance Facet**: (Canonical, Certified, Experimental)
- **Security Facet**: (Restricted, Public)

*Ontology Type (Level 2) is simply the set of values applied to these Facets.*

## 3. Selective First-Class Edges
Relations (edges) are not uniformly heavy. They are bifurcated to balance mathematical power with computational practicality.
- **First-Class Edges**: Relations that carry provenance, history, and confidence. They possess their own Identities. Reserved for epistemic and causal links (`supports`, `contradicts`, `derived_from`, `equivalent_to`).
- **Structural Edges**: Lightweight topological links without independent identities (`part_of`, `instance_of`, `contains`).

## 4. The Anatomy of Evaluation
There is no absolute "Truth." There is only the *Current Best Supported Interpretation under Evaluation Policy P*.
$$Assessment = Evaluation\_Function \times Evaluation\_Context \times Evidence\_Set$$
