# Foundation Zero: Omni Audit Execution Plan

## Objective
The primary objective of the Omni Audit is **NOT** to break the repository, but to **attempt to force a constitutional amendment**. Every finding must be evaluated to determine if it can be solved in Levels 2-3 (Ontology/Implementation) or if it exposes a fatal flaw in Levels 0-1 (The Constitution).

## Things the Constitution Refuses to Solve (Non-Goals)
To prevent scope creep, the Constitution explicitly refuses to govern:
- What is morally true.
- Business priorities.
- Resolution of scientific controversies.
- Inference of missing evidence.
- Guaranteeing semantic equivalence.
- Eliminating human judgment.

---

## The Stages of Auditing

### Stage 0: Constitutional Falsification
*Goal: Prove Constitution v1.0 is internally inconsistent using logic alone.*
- Can two constitutional laws contradict?
- Can one law imply another (redundancy)?
- Are hidden assumptions violating the Law of Explicitness?

### Stage 1: Constitutional & Ontology Stress Test
*Goal: Stress Levels 1 & 2.*
- Can the ontology scale? Is there semantic leakage?

### Stage 2: Knowledge & Governance Audit
*Goal: Stress Levels 3 & 5.*
- Epistemic consistency, multi-agent ledger corruption, governance bloat.

### Stage 3: Engineering & Operations Audit
*Goal: Stress Levels 4 & 6.*
- Active breakage: corrupt schemas, duplicate IDs.

### Stage 4: Reality & Migration Mathematics
*Goal: Stress Level 7.*
- The 300-page PDF test. Idempotency proofs.

---

## The Adversary: The Malicious but Compliant Agent
The audit will emulate an agent that never violates rules but exploits ambiguity:
- Creates misleading but technically valid provenance.
- Manufactures ontology edge cases.
- Produces semantically equivalent but operationally divergent representations.
- Causes uncertainty propagation explosions.

---

## Scoring & Classification Matrix
Every finding will be classified using the **Constitution vs Reality Matrix**:
- Constitution Failure
- Ontology Failure
- Implementation Failure
- Data Quality / Operator / Environmental Failure
