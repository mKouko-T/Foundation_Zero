# Design Axioms

These 12 axioms are the enduring, mathematical truths of Foundation Zero. Everything else derives from them.

1. **Evidence precedes interpretation.**
2. **Every permanent artifact has an owner.**
3. **Mutable state is a cache until proven otherwise.**
4. **Identity is eternal; assessments are versioned.**
5. **Verification is stronger than assertion.**
6. **Architecture follows repeated implementation.**
7. **Unknown is an acceptable result.**
8. **Human judgment is authoritative but should be evidenced.**
9. **Deletion is a first-class engineering operation.**
10. **Simplicity is achieved by removing decisions, not hiding them.**
11. **A projection may be deleted without data loss.**
12. **Rules outlive implementations.**
