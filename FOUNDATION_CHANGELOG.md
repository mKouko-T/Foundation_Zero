# Foundation Zero Changelog

**Purpose:** This log records the empirical history of Foundation Zero. Every entry here represents an architectural or doctrinal evolution forced by reality, not by speculative engineering.

Foundation Zero does not learn from itself. It learns from execution friction in downstream repositories.

## Entry Format
```markdown
### [Date] - [Version Bump]

- **Reality Event:** [What happened in the real world / downstream repo?]
- **Problem:** [What specific friction or failure did this cause?]
- **Evidence:** [Where is the proof of this failure?]
- **Foundation Change:** [What was changed in the Foundation to prevent this?]
- **Downstream Effect:** [What happened after the change?]
- **Was it worth it?** [Yes/No/Pending]
```

---

### 2026-07-13 - v1.0.0 (Genesis)
- **Reality Event:** Completion of the incubation phase.
- **Problem:** The system was recursively learning from its own archives rather than business reality.
- **Evidence:** Chat_001 accumulated 106 chunks of unstructured conversation, causing epistemic drag and architectural bloat.
- **Foundation Change:** Enforced the Global Release Gate, froze the architectural baseline, and mandated that Foundation Zero no longer learns from itself.
- **Downstream Effect:** Cornerstone was unblocked to initialize from zero.
- **Was it worth it?** Pending validation by Cornerstone execution.
